FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About PostOutLoud

Who owns the content posted on PostOutLoud?

All content posted on PostOutLoud is owned by the original poster. Please reach out to the original poster for permission before reproducing content elsewhere.

Who owns the source code used in PostOutLoud?

The code for this website was written by Jennifer & Sakura from Girls Who Code!

Who is responsible for inaccurate and/or inappropriate material posted on PostOutLoud?

Just as users of PostOutLoud hold the rights to what they post on PostOutLoud, they are also responsible for ensuring that the text and/or images and/or videos that they post on this website is accurate, appropriate, and aligned with the purpose of this website.

So what is PostOutLoud for?

PostOutLoud is a place for people to get together from all around the world to post about and discuss social justice issues within their own communities.

What sort of audience is PostOutLoud for>

PostOutLoud is intended for all audiences who are interested in engaging in discussions regarding social justice. It is a place for people to discuss their real-life experiences of homophobia, racism, sexism, transphobia, etc. and the ways in which the intersectionalities of all of these issues affect peoples' lives today.

How do I contact the creators of PostOutLoud?

You can contact the creators using the form on our contact page.
